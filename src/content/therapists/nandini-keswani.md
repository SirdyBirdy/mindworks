Nandini works with people carrying things that happened a long time ago and are still making noise today. Her approach is slow and careful — she doesn't rush toward solutions.

She is trained in trauma-informed CBT and grief counselling, and brings a quiet steadiness to sessions that people tend to find settling. She works well with people who have found other therapy too fast, too directive, or too focused on fixing.

Her sessions often involve understanding how the past is showing up in the present — and creating a little distance between the two.
