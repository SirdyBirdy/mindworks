Dimple started Mindworks in 2016 after years of noticing that most therapy spaces felt clinical and distant. She wanted something different: a small, honest practice where people could actually say the thing they had been sitting on.

She works primarily with adults navigating burnout, relationship stress, and life transitions. Her approach draws from CBT, ACT, and psychodynamic thinking — but in practice, it looks more like a very structured conversation than a textbook session.

She has been told she asks the questions people did not know they needed to hear.
