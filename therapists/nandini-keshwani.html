About

A bit about Nandini

Nandini works with people who are figuring out who they are, what they want, and how to feel okay in their own skin. She brings warmth and a non-judgmental presence to questions around body image, identity, and growth.

Her sessions are unhurried and grounded. She believes that feeling better is possible, and that the work of getting there is worth taking slowly.

Specialties

What she works with

  • Body image & self-esteem
  • Queer identity & self-acceptance
  • Anxiety & social anxiety
  • Young adult transitions
  • Family & relationship dynamics
  • Personal growth & self-worth
Approach

How she works

Nandini draws from person-centred and humanistic traditions, with practical CBT tools where helpful. She works collaboratively — you bring the direction, she helps you walk it.

She pays attention to what's not being said as much as what is. In her experience, the things people hesitate to bring up are often the most important ones.

FAQ

Common questions

I'm not sure I have anything "serious enough" to see a therapist for.
You don't need to be in crisis to benefit from therapy. If something's been on your mind, or you've been going in circles, that's enough. A lot of the most useful work happens before things get to a breaking point.
What age group do you work with?
Primarily young adults and adults — roughly 18 to 40. I assess each enquiry individually, so if you're outside that range, feel free to reach out and we can discuss whether it's a good fit.
Do you offer sessions in Marathi?
Yes. If Marathi feels more natural for expressing certain things, that's completely fine. Sessions can be a mix of languages if that works better for you.
